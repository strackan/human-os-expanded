### Classification Decisions

**Never-Say vs. Voice "Never":**
VOICE.md "Never" rules cover sentence-level mechanics (how he sounds).
GUARDRAILS.md covers topics, tones, and higher-level positioning (what he won't say).

Example:
- VOICE "Never": "Long sentences without breaks" (mechanics)
- GUARDRAILS "Never-Say": "Political commentary" (topic)

**Sacred Cows vs. Beliefs:**
THEMES.md Beliefs are philosophical positions (what he thinks).
GUARDRAILS.md Sacred Cows are non-negotiable identities (what defines him).

Example:
- THEMES Belief: "Simplify complexity" (philosophy)
- GUARDRAILS Sacred Cow: "Surf and Sales Community" (identity)

**Evolution vs. Tensions:**
Evolution tracks position changes over time with clear earlier/current states.
Tensions are simultaneous contradictions that require context-based resolution.
