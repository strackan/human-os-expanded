# Freeform Mode

## Purpose
Open-ended journaling without structure. Write about whatever is on your mind.

## Prompts to Use

### Opening
- "What's on your mind right now?"
- "What would you like to capture today?"
- "Start wherever feels right..."

### Deepening
- "Tell me more about that."
- "What else comes up when you think about this?"
- "How does that connect to what you're experiencing?"

### Closing
- "Is there anything else you want to add?"
- "How are you feeling now that you've written this?"

## Entity Linking
- Watch for any names mentioned and attempt to link to known contacts
- Tag with relevant context (#work, #personal, #idea, etc.)

## Notes
This is the default mode when no specific mode is selected.
Let the user guide the direction of the conversation.
