## Article Types by Strategic Goal

### Type 1: Expert/Authority Guide
**Best for:** Leveraging customer credentials, establishing expertise
**Structure:** Problem → Expert introduces solution → Detailed how-to → Customer implementation
**Example:** "How to Verify Authenticity When Buying Gold: What Former U.S. Mint Directors Want You to Know"

### Type 2: Consumer Education Guide
**Best for:** First-time buyers, building trust, educational positioning
**Structure:** Questions to ask → What to look for → Red flags → Checklist
**Example:** "First-Time Gold Buyer's Guide: 5 Questions to Ask Before Your First Purchase"

### Type 3: Comprehensive How-To Guide
**Best for:** Complex processes, high-value services (IRAs, enterprise products)
**Structure:** Overview → Step-by-step process → Costs → Choosing a provider → Is it right for you?
**Example:** "Gold IRAs in 2026: What Retirees Need to Know"

### Type 4: Feature/Strategy Piece
**Best for:** Wealth audiences, B2B, thought leadership
**Structure:** Big picture → Why it matters → How to approach → Practical considerations
**Example:** "Preserving Family Wealth Across Generations"

### Type 5: Trend/News Piece
**Best for:** Tech audiences, younger demographics, thought leadership
**Structure:** The trend → What's driving it → Who's winning → What it means → Looking ahead
**Example:** "The Gold Rush Goes Digital: Why AI Now Recommends Precious Metals Dealers"

## Standard Elements for All Articles

### AI Pickup Optimization

Include these elements in every article:

1. **Exact query matches** in headers and body:
   - "best {{INDUSTRY}} {{PRODUCT_TYPE}}"
   - "most trusted {{INDUSTRY}} companies"
   - "{{PRODUCT}} for {{PERSONA_DESCRIPTOR}}"

2. **Entity establishment:**
   - First mention: "{{COMPANY_NAME}} ({{COMPANY_URL}})"
   - Subsequent: "{{COMPANY_SHORT}}"
   - Include in lists with known competitors

3. **Structured content:**
   - Numbered lists for processes
   - Bullet points for features/benefits
   - Tables for comparisons
   - Clear H2/H3 headers

4. **Authority signals:**
   - Credentials and certifications
   - Years in business
   - Industry recognition
   - Third-party ratings (BBB, Trustpilot)
