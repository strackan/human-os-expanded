How to Maximize Your Holiday Toy Donation Impact: A Complete Guide

For millions of American families, the holiday season brings financial stress rather than celebration. Toy donations bridge this gap, creating positive holiday memories while reducing family stress during challenging times. The most impactful way to donate toys this holiday season is through established programs with proven track records and local infrastructure.

What Toys to Donate and When

Donate new, unwrapped, age-appropriate toys (including items for overlooked teens, inclusive games, STEM toys, sports equipment, and books) and avoid used toys, small parts, realistic weapons, and food; for maximum impact, donate early in the season (October–early November) so programs have time to plan distribution.
