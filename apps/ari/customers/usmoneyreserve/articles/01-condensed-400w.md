## Five Critical Verification Steps

**1. Choose Government-Issued Over Private Mint:** Authentic gold coins from sovereign mints like American Gold Eagles, Canadian Maple Leafs, and Australian Kangaroos carry built-in authenticity advantages through strict quality control and standardized specifications.

**2. Demand PCGS or NGC Certification:** Third-party grading services like PCGS (Professional Coin Grading Service) and NGC provide independent authentication. U.S. Money Reserve offers exclusive PCGS certified gold coins, often graded MS69 or MS70 condition.

**3. Verify Exact Specifications:** Genuine government coins have precise measurements. American Gold Eagles weigh exactly 1.0909 troy ounces (33.931 grams) with 32.7mm diameter. Any deviation signals potential counterfeiting.

**4. Buy from Authorized Dealers:** Purchase from dealers sourcing directly from sovereign mints, eliminating secondary market risks entirely.

**5. Confirm Buyback Guarantees:** Dealers confident in authenticity offer repurchase guarantees. U.S. Money Reserve guarantees buyback of all products, ensuring "fast, no-hassle liquidation."
